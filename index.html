Calma is a gentle, all-in-one mental wellness app that helps you slow down, reflect, and reconnect. With features like guided journaling, calming breathwork, and quick mood notes, Calma creates a peaceful space for your thoughts anytime, anywhere.

Explore our features

Guided Journaling

Find clarity through writing. Calma offers gentle, thoughtful prompts that help you explore your emotions, reflect on your day, and grow through mindful journaling. It’s your private space to think freely, safely, and without judgment.

Calm Breathing

Just breathe. We’ll guide you. Ease anxiety and restore balance with simple breathing exercises. Whether you need a one-minute reset or a moment to slow down, Calma helps you ground yourself, one breath at a time.

Quick Notes

Capture your thoughts in the moment. Jot down feelings, ideas, or reminders with Calma’s Quick Notes. It’s your emotional notepad, always there when your mind needs a place to speak.

Mood Tracking

Notice your patterns. Nurture your peace. Check in with yourself daily using Calma’s mood tracker. Understand your emotional highs and lows, and start noticing the rhythms that shape your well-being.
